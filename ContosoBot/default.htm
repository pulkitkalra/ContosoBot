ContosoBot

The ContosoBot is for customers of the Contoso Bank.

Some of the things you can ask the bot:

  • To get a stock price by its stock symbol: "Give me the msft stock price".
  • To make a particular stock your favourite: "make msft my favourite stock".
  • To clear your favourite stock: "clear favourite stock".
  • To exchange x amount of abc currency to xyz currency "convert 1 nzd to usd".
  • To report a stolen card: "report card 1651 as stolen".
  • To see list of stolen/lost cards: "show list of stolen and lost cards".
  • To mark a card as found: "mark card 1651 as found".

Note that all the commands above can made in several different ways, as the bot is designed to understand natural language
